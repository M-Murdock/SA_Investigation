This study will take approximately 15-20 minutes and consists of:

  • An interactive simulation task

Study Information

Purpose: This study investigates how people interact with AI assistance systems that infer user goals and provide collaborative support.

What you'll do: You will control a cursor using arrow keys to move toward different goals. An AI system will observe your movements and provide assistance based on its inference of your intended goal.

Data Collection: We will collect:

  • Anonymous demographic information
  • Your interactions with the simulator (movements, timing, actions)
  • Your responses to survey questions

Privacy: All data is stored anonymously using only your assigned User ID. No personally identifiable information will be collected.

Voluntary: Your participation is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

Note: Please complete the study in one sitting. Your User ID will be used to link your responses across all parts of the study.
