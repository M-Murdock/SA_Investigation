Shared-control cooking environment

Your Task Explore the environment

Use the arrow keys to move the spoon. You will be sharing control over the spoon with an agent (represented by the robot gripper) to interact with a cooking environment.

In each of these simulations, you will be using one of six different teleoperation systems. In each, the agent will try to assist you by guiding the motion of the spoon towards one of 3 cooking actions that it believes you are trying to perform.

You do not have to complete any particular task but your goal is to identify these 3 actions based on the agent's guidance.
